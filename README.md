**Strong Monthly Growth Pattern**
Monthly revenue increases steadily from January to June, indicating rising customer demand and successful product-market fit. May and June show the highest sales, suggesting seasonal or promotional impacts.

**Peak Selling Days**
Sales remain relatively consistent through the week, but Thursday, Friday, and Monday record slightly higher total bills. Weekend traffic is stable but not significantly higher, showing weekday-driven coffee demand.

**High-Performing Hours**

Hourly quantity trends show a clear morning-to-midday peak, with the strongest sales between 8 AM and 12 PM, matching typical coffee consumer behavior. A gradual decline after afternoon hours indicates limited evening demand.
